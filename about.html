About CompliTrack SA

CompliTrack SA is a web-based employee compliance platform that helps companies store and manage employee information digitally.

Problem

Many businesses still use paper files, lose documents, forget expiry dates and struggle during inspections.

Solution

The website provides a secure dashboard where HR can manage employees, upload documents, track expiry dates and generate reports.
